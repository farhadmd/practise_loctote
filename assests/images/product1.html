The LOCTOTE® Cinch Pack seemed like an obvious name to us for a backpack that is literally a “cinch” to use, and is used by "cinching" it. It's a tough, practical, daily backpack that's built with our flagship security components. A hardwearing and timeless piece that is fabricated from premium waxed canvas and sustainable full-grain leather, this bag doesn’t just carry your belongings, it protects them in style.

• Patented double steel reinforced locking strap & heavy-duty attached combination lock allow you to lock the bag shut, and to a fixed object

• Lightweight slash, tear & abrasion resistant fabric with industrial water resistant exterior coating

• UHMWPE locking straps reinforced with a steel-reinforced inner core doubles as the backpack straps

• Large fleece-lined RFID blocking internal pocket

• Hidden easy-access exterior pocket with YKK zipper 

• Two high-capacity water bottle pockets 

  • 20" x 13" x  6" (fits Macbook Pro 15 inch)

  • Capacity: 22 litres

  • 2.6 lbs (includes lock)

  • Interior Pocket: 9" x 8.5" (can fit an iPad Mini)

  • Natural leather accents

  • Designed in Columbus. Manufactured in China.

Designed to Protect,
Built to Last

Whether you’re headed to school, the office, the gym, the beach, or traveling the world — this lockable day pack will protect your stuff from thieves, and you from bland accessories.

SECURE LOCKING SYSTEM

Easily lock your bag to any fixed object for peace of mind using the patent-pending locking strap. It's reinforced with stainless steel, and assembled with solid brass components, making it difficult to cut, break or tear without extreme force or heavy tools.

SLASH RESISTANT FABRIC

This bag achieves Level 4 cut protection and is crafted from woven layers of state-of-the-art fibers, including ultra high molecular weight polyethylene (UHMWPE).

SLASH RESISTANT STRAPS

Made with a cut resistant core interwoven with highly-reflective material for night visibility. Even though we hope no thief ever tries to cut this bag off your back, it’s nice to know they won’t be successful.

RFID BLOCKING TECHNOLOGY

Protect against RFID electronic pick-pocketing. This sack has an internal pocket lined with a certified RFID blocking material to shield credit cards & passports from unauthorized scanning.

WATER RESISTANT

Our bags are lined with a water resistant nylon lining and is equipped with a large tablet-sized water-resistant interior pocket crafted with an Aquaguard zipper to keep your stuff as dry as possible.

HIDDEN BACK POCKET

Easy access hidden back pocket to store your essentials when you need them at a moments notice.

SLASH RESISTANT (CUT LEVEL 4)

Our slash-resistant toughness comes from the superior cut protection of Honeywell's UHMWPE Spectra fiber, the same fiber used in police vests and military gear for bullet resistance. Feel the peace of mind from knowing your belongings are protected by this stuff.
